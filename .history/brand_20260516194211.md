# Brand — SafeChain MK

_Status: active_

Elevated institutional design system for the SafeChain MK anti-phishing traffic-fine
portal. Light, trustworthy, and immersive through depth, atmosphere and choreographed
motion — never through darkness or crypto flash. The portal must always read as an
official government service.

## Palette

- Base background — `#e9eef6`, carrying a soft atmospheric radial wash
- Surface / cards — `#ffffff` with layered soft shadows (`shadow-card`, `shadow-lift`)
- Hero / dark surfaces — navy ramp `#0a1f3c` → `#081628` → `#050d1c`
- Primary accent — trust blue `#2563eb` / `#1d4ed8`
- Semantic — emerald (success / safe), amber (warning), red (danger / scam)
- Solana mark — gradient `#9945ff` → `#14f195`, used only for the on-chain identity
- Text — `slate-900` headings, `slate-600` body, `slate-400/500` muted

## Typography

- Display — Space Grotesk: wordmark, headings, large monetary amounts
- Body / UI — Inter
- Mono — JetBrains Mono: security codes, hashes, signatures, plates, coordinates

## Motion

- Choreographed scroll-reveal: 18px rise + fade, staggered per section
- Micro-interactions 100–150ms, `ease-out` on enter / `ease-in` on leave
- Every animation respects `prefers-reduced-motion`

## Voice

- Calm, official, reassuring. Active voice. Concise.
- Bilingual: Macedonian (primary) and English, full parity.
- The anti-phishing principle is reinforced throughout: never a link, always a code.
